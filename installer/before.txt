Before You Install PiXELL-River Account Manager

Thank you for choosing PiXELL-River Account Manager! Before we proceed with the installation, please ensure the following requirements are met:

1. System Requirements:
    - Operating System: Windows 10 or newer
    - RAM: Minimum 4GB
    - Disk Space: Minimum 20GB free space
    - An active internet connection for activation

2. Please close all other applications before starting the installation. This will help in updating system files without interruptions.

3. Ensure you have administrative rights on your computer to install software.

Proceed with the installation by clicking 'Next'. We hope you enjoy using our software!
